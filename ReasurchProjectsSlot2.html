Description

In this lab, we used a tensile tester on 5052 aluminum alloy, cold-rolled AISI 1010 plain carbon steel alloy, and heat-treated AISI 1010 plain carbon steel alloy to determine and calculate the Youngs modulus and the vital elements of the stress and strain graph in each material. I worked with a small cross-functional team to break down the tasks to create this lab report, conduct the necessary calculations, and record the data. We used Excel to track and record data and create the graphs shown in the report.
