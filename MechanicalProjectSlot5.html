Description

In this project, I 3D-modeled each component in Solidworks and created an assembly. I also designed a functional circuit diagram for the electrical components and hand-soldered all components together. I used my material science and manufacturing knowledge to craft the axial shaft and spacers. I then used CNC machining and 3D printing to create the Battery housing, main body, and wheels out of Polycarbonate, Nylon, and PLC. Polycarbonate was used for the main body due to its durability as it collides at a reasonably high velocity against walls. Nylon was selected for the wheels due to its reduced conductivity to avoid damaging any electrical components.
