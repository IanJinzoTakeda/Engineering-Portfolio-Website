Description

One of my tasks for the university of Waterloos Baja SEA design team I designed a Gimbal to safely secure a Go-pro, which is meant to attach to the Vehicle and capture and record surounding enviornmental data with 180 degrees along the x and Z axis.

Inorder to determine which material it is most sutible to print the gimabal in, I had to consider the stress and strain of each conetion point of the gimbal. I concluded that the best material to construct the gimbal from should be ASA (Acrylonitrile Styrene Acrylate) as it has resistance to UV radiation, avoiding degradation and has comparible strength to ABS (Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene). ASA at a 25% fill privded substatial stregnth against the envornment and was strong enough to endure testing of teh Baje SAE Buggy.
